Books Books The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho Genre: Inspirational Fiction / Adventure Details:
A philosophical novel about a young Andalusian shepherd named Santiago who dreams of finding a worldly treasure. The story explores themes of destiny, personal growth, and the importance of following one's dreams. Written in a simple yet profound style, it has become a global bestseller and is often recommended for young adults. Originally published in Portuguese (O Alquimista) in 1988.
